HOW TO BAKE A CHOCOLATE CAKE

Easy Chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients

For the sponge:

  • 220g unsalted butter, softened
  • 220g caster suger
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2tbsp milk
  • a small pinch of salt

For the ganache:

  • 300ml double cream
  • 300g dark chocolate
